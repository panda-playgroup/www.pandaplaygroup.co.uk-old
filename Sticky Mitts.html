Sticky Mitts

Sticky Mitts and Tiny Tots is a friendly, fun group for children and their grown-ups on a Monday and Wednesday morning 9.45-11.15, during term time.

Meetings take place in the Methodist Hall, Main Street Copmanthorpe YO23 3SU

Babies and toddlers from 0 - 4+ Years are welcomed

We offer playing, crafting, getting messy, a healthy snack and, of course, singing! We also have baby weighing scales.

Covid Risk Assessments have been undertaken and all government regulations rigorously followed.

The sessions are £3 for one adult and one child or £4 for an adult and more than one child.

We ask for registration, booking and prepayment for half a term in advance.

Please email pandaplaygroup@hotmail.co.uk for more information and to register
