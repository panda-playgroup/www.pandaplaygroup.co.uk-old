Whitebox

Thanks for visiting the website for the Panda Preschool and Whitebox photoshoot, taking place at Howell Hall,YO23 3SQ. This year we will be hosting the event on Sunday 14th November. Each session will run for 10 minutes (single booking - £35) or for 25 minutes (double booking - £65). After each shoot customers will be provided with instructions on how to access all images taken via a secure online portal, all images can then be downloaded and used as you see fit.

COVID

Obviously, COVID has impacted all aspects of daily life over the last year or so and we wanted to reassure you that we are doing everything we can to ensure the safety of the photographers and our guests.

  • Pre-COVID we ran two photoshoots simultaneously, this year to ensure everyone has their own space we will be running one shoot.
  • The photographers will be more than happy to offer advice and guidance on how participants should pose, however, they will be unable to approach you as they must maintain social distance.
  • Whitebox will be unable to provide any props or costumes, but are more than happy for you to bring your own.
  • Owing to social bubbles, many schools are unable to photograph siblings from different year groups at the same time. If you so wish, children are invited to attend in school uniform so that siblings can be photographed together.
  • Groups will have to be kept to a maximum of six, there will be one member of Panda staff in attendance along with the photographer, who will maintain social distance.
  • If government guidance changes and the event needs to be postponed, or if a family needs to self isolate, a full refund will be made available.
  • All details will be kept for three weeks in the interests of track and trace.
  • Each single or double booking is followed by a five minute break to allow for cleaning. We therefore ask that you arrive and leave promptly. If participants are late, unless the next slot is free, we will be unable to extend your booking.

Please do get in touch if you have any other questions - The Panda Team!
