Paying For Your Childcare

Invoices are issued on a half-termly basis and we accept childcare vouchers (from Computershare Voucher Services, Edenred Childcare Vouchers, KiddiVouchers and Fideliti), cash and cheques.

Note: Nursery Education Funding is available from the term following a child's third birthday. Please visit the YorOK Website for further details.

Please contact the vice-treasurer with any questions regarding fees and funding.
